Dakop-Dakop

Traditional Filipino Tag Game

How to Play Dakop-Dakop

Basic Rules:

  • Use WASD or Arrow keys to move your player
  • Chasers (red) try to tag runners (blue)
  • Avoid being tagged by staying away from chasers
  • Use walls and obstacles as cover

Single Chaser Mode:

  • One player is "it" at the start
  • When tagged, you become the new chaser
  • Tag-back rule: You can immediately tag back the previous chaser
  • Game continues until time runs out

Multi-Chaser Mode:

  • Tagged players become additional chasers
  • Game ends when all players are tagged
  • Ranking based on order of being tagged (last = 1st place)
  • No tag-back rule in this mode

Local Filipino Twist:

  • "Langit-lupa" safe zones (elevated areas in blue)
  • Limited time in safe zones (3 seconds max)
  • "Freeze" command - all players stop for 2 seconds
  • Power-ups: Speed boost and temporary invincibility
